Bio

I am a PhD student in Civil Engineering and Transport at Gdańsk University of Technology, specializing in urban flood risk assessment and management in the context of climate change. My research focuses on integrating green infrastructure solutions with advanced GIS, remote sensing, and machine learning techniques to enhance flood resilience in urban environments.
